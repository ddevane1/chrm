An all-Ireland initiative building evidence synthesis knowledge, awareness and capacity. We provide training, fellowships, and produce high-quality systematic reviews and rapid evidence syntheses for organisations including WHO and ECDC. ESI hosts Cochrane Ireland, promoting evidence-informed health decision-making.

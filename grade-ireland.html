About GRADE Ireland

Supporting healthcare professionals and researchers in evidence-based decision making

What is GRADE?

GRADE (Grading of Recommendations Assessment, Development and Evaluation) is a transparent framework for developing and presenting summaries of evidence and provides a systematic approach for making clinical practice recommendations.

Our Mission

The GRADE Ireland Network aims to build capacity and expertise in the use of GRADE methodology across Ireland, supporting healthcare professionals, researchers, and policymakers in making evidence-informed decisions.

Network Activities

We provide training workshops, mentorship programs, and resources to support the implementation of GRADE methodology in systematic reviews, clinical guidelines, and health technology assessments.

Training & Support

Workshops

One-on-one mentorship and support for researchers and teams implementing GRADE in their systematic reviews and guideline development projects.
